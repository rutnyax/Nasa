Bank PO

Our Bank PO classroom course helps the students in preparing for various Bank PO Entrance exams conducted by different banks across India. It is a program of 13 weeksâ€™ duration that is designed for students who require exposure to fundamental concepts and adequate practice for preparation.

Our Bank PO classroom course prepares students in areas like Quantitative Aptitude (including Data Interpretation & Data Sufficiency), Reasoning & Logical Ability (including Non-Verbal Reasoning), English Language & Grammar, General Awareness (including socio-economic & financial awareness), Marketing Aptitude and Computer Knowledge. It is preparation in these areas that is crucial for selection in Bank PO exams. Apart from the classroom training, the students will also be given study material that includes booklets for fundamentals apart from Class Exercises and Mock Tests.

Salient features of our Bank Clerical Course:

1) Over 200 hours of intensive Classroom Training Programme
2) Weekly Tests and Full-Length Tests
3) Full-Length Mocks based on both IBPS and SBI PO exam patterns
4) Latest and updated Notes and Tests on GK & Current Affairs
5) Support for Group Discussion & Personal Interview for students qualifying written exam
6) Special tutorial classes to discuss doubts of students
