B.Ed Entrance

After completing graduation you need to enroll for B. Ed. i.e. Bachelor of Education Program in a recognized university in order to pursue your career to be a School Teacher. At this point the real challenge starts, thousands of candidates apply for B.Ed. regular course in reputed institutions. To select right candidate universities conduct entrance exams for B.Ed regular course program. You need to clear this entrance exam to secure your seat in reputed institute ,NASA Academy having a couple years of successful experience can be your destination for preparation of B. Ed. Entrance Exams.

With expert faculties and dedicated management staff NASA Academy has become the first choice among students appearing for B. Ed. Entrance exams. We have flexible batch timings considering college schedules of the students at all of our centers.

B.Ed Entrance Eligibility :

The candidates should have passed Higher Secondary/ Graduation with minimum 50 % marks from a recognized University.

B.Ed Exam Syllabus :

Main content of the B.Ed Entrance exam are as follow:

1) Numerical Ability
2) G.K and Metal Ability
3) English
4) Teaching Potential
5) Language Test

B.Ed Exam Dates :

B.ED entrance is usually conducted in the month of July.
